Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

Projects can be cancelled with 30 days written notice. Cancellation terms may vary based on project stage and deliverables completed.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis depending on project completion status and deliverables provided.

3. Refund Process

Refund requests must be submitted in writing with detailed justification. Processing time is typically 15-30 business days.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Third-party licenses, completed deliverables, and consultation hours are generally non-refundable.

5. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be available based on work completed and resources allocated to the project.

6. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes regarding refunds will be resolved through mutual discussion and, if necessary, mediation.

7. Contact for Refunds

For refund requests, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 9818280398 with your project details and reason for refund.
